Okonomiyaki

Description

Okonomiyaki is a savory Japanese cabbage pancake.

In the U.S., people sometimes call it a “Japanese pizza” or “Japanese frittata” because of its customizable toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp Diamond Crystal kosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 5.6 oz mountain yam
  • 3/4 cup dashi
  • 1/2 head green cabbage
  • 1/4 cup pickled ginger
  • 1/2 lb sliced pork belly
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup tenkasu
  • neutral oil
  • okonomiyaki sauce
  • toppings of your choice

Steps

  1. If time allows allow batter to rest overnight
  2. Combine the flour, salt, sugar and baking powder in a bowl
  3. Peel the mountain yam
  4. Add the mountain yam and the dashi
  5. Mix all of it together and allow the batter to rest overnight
  6. Discard the core of the green cabbage and cut it into thin slices
  7. Mince the cabbage strips
  8. Chop the pickled ginger
  9. Add eggs and tenkasu to the batter
  10. Add the cabbage to the batter
  11. Check the consistency of the batter
  12. Heat oil in a pan
  13. Spread and shape the batter into a circle
  14. Place 3 slices of pork belly on top of the batter
  15. When it is nicely browned on the bottom, flip it over
  16. Gently press the okonomiyaki
  17. Flip one last time
  18. Transfer it onto a plate
