French Onion Soup

Description

French onion soup is a classic French dish made with caramalized onions.

The soup is topped with bread and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 4 cups of sliced onions
  • 5 cups of beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons of dry sherry
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 pinch of salt and pepper
  • 4 slices of French bread
  • 4 slices of provolone cheese
  • 2 slices of Swiss cheese
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients
  2. Melt butter with olive oil
  3. Add beef broth, sherry and thyme
  4. Ladle soup into oven-safe serving bowls and place on slice of bread on top with cheese
  5. Broil in the oven
  6. Serve hot and enjoy
